About Me

I earned my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Cornell University under the mentorship of Professor Carla P. Gomes. My research expertise lies in the realms of artificial intelligence and machine learning, with a particular emphasis on integrating prior knowledge-based logical and constraint reasoning with advanced deep learning techniques. My work encompasses solving complex problems in structured prediction, multi-entity modeling, covariate shift, and task-based learning.

Post-graduation, I joined Deep Reasoning AI Inc., where I served as the Director of Research and Engineering and led Deep Reasoning AI’s research and development of AI for Rapid Personalized 3D Anatomical Modeling.

Apart from my professional pursuits, I am a big fan in competitive programming, and our team was the champion in ACM-ICPC Great New York Regional and proceeded to compete in the 42nd ACM-ICPC World Finals. My foundational education in this field was at the Zhiyuan College of Shanghai Jiaotong University, China, where I graduated with a B.Sc. in 2017 from the ACM honored Class.
